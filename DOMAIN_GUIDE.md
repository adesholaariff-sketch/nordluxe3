## 💡 Domain Name Tips

### ✅ Do's:
- Keep it short and memorable
- Use keywords related to your business
- Choose .com if available and affordable
- Make it easy to spell
- Consider brandability

### ❌ Don'ts:
- Don't use numbers unless part of brand
- Avoid hyphens (nord-luxe vs nordluxe)
- Don't choose extremely long domains
- Avoid trademark infringement
- Don't rush - take time to choose well

### Pro Tips:
- **Check social media**: Ensure matching handles available
- **Mobile friendly**: Test typing on mobile keyboard
- **Pronunciation**: Should be clear how to say it
- **Future-proof**: Consider if it will work as you scale
- **Legal check**: Ensure no trademark conflicts
